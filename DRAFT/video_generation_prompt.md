## 主提示词

Create a 40-second cinematic concept trailer for the game "Nestbreakers / 破巢者" using the 10 supplied keyframes as strict visual anchors in order. The film is a fixed-oblique 2.5D looter-shooter survival concept: one lone hero breaks into an enemy nest, survives hordes and traps, steals power, and shatters the nest core.

Visual style: high-end stylized game trailer concept art, cinematic but gameplay-readable, fixed 3/4 oblique camera language for interior combat shots, not photorealistic documentary, not anime, not pixel art. Maintain strong continuity of the same lone Nestbreaker hero: black-metal armor, violet and amber-gold accents, compact rifle, heroic but small against the hostile environment. The hero and player bullets must avoid cyan, red, and white as main colors.

Enemy nest art direction: the nest is calcified living architecture created by alien inter-universe rules, like biological industry rather than a wet hive. Use bone-white calcified plates, wax-yellow shell layers, dry flesh membranes, deep red fissures, black-purple holes and depth, with very sparse cyan reserved only for nest-core energy, elite weak points, nest terminals, and key enemy-system signals. Avoid wet gore, slime, blood-soaked surfaces, generic xenomorph corridors, and all-cyan sci-fi lighting.

Color language: player fire is gold / amber with violet accents; enemy ranged threats and hazard warnings are red / deep red; cyan belongs to enemy weak points and the nest core; reward objects use independent function colors: chest gold + emerald, relic altar purple / blue-violet, upgrade station orange / amber.

Camera and motion: begin with a wide space establishing shot, push toward the enemy nest, then transition into fixed-oblique arena shots. Use slow cinematic pushes for keyframes 01-03, sharper action motion for keyframes 04 and 08, a brief slower reward beat for keyframe 05, precise dodge timing for keyframe 06, a dramatic low-to-oblique reveal for keyframe 07, a grand final-objective push for keyframe 09, and a cathartic core-collapse pullback for keyframe 10. Keep the central combat area readable and avoid excessive camera shake.

Narrative beats: capital sector still fights; one hero invades instead of defending; the nest is alive but industrial; combat is shooting-first; rewards and builds matter; traps are readable; elite weak points use cyan; overdrive shooting creates a power spike; the nest core is the final target; shattering the core is a temporary victory inside a larger war.

Constraints: no on-screen text, no subtitles, no logo, no watermark, no UI overlays, no readable signage, no gore splatter, no wet organic slime, no comedic tone, no generic military bunker look, no random extra heroes, no player cyan/red/white bullets, no all-assets-diamond design. Diamond shapes are only for ground hazards, AOE, floor-grid alignment, and tactical readability.
